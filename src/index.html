Register your guests for eVisitor
without losing your afternoon.

Fill in a simple form for each guest. When you're done, save the file to your computer and upload it on eVisitor under Turisti → Prijava putem datoteke. Everything stays in your browser — nothing is sent to us or anywhere else.

  1. 1 Your property Tell us your eVisitor facility code once. We'll remember it on this device.
  2. 2 Your guests Add a quick row for each overnight visitor — name, passport, dates.
  3. 3 Save and upload Save the file, log into eVisitor, upload it. That's the whole job.
